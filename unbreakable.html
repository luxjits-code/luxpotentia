The curriculum

Nine modules.
One outcome.

Module I
You Are Not Helpless

The moment everything changes. You'll learn the guillotine choke — simple, devastating, and the technique that will make you realize this stuff is real. We start here because belief is the foundation of everything that follows.

Module II
Why Women Freeze

The neuroscience of the freeze response — why it's not your fault, and how training literally rewires it. Every repetition builds a map your nervous system can follow under pressure, before conscious thought even kicks in.

Module III
Becoming Harder to Choose

What convicted violent offenders look for when selecting a target — it's not size or age, it's certainty. The 1981 Grayson & Stein study, Ted Bundy's own words, and how to carry yourself like someone no one wants to test.

Module IV
Verbal Boundaries & Presence

Your voice is a weapon. How to use it loudly and unapologetically, direct bystanders, and interrupt an attack psychologically before it becomes physical. Verbal boundary training that overrides freeze before anything gets physical.

Module V
Standing Defense: Grabs & Control

Wrist grabs, hair grabs, shirt grabs, attempts to drag you to a second location — this is where most attacks begin. Techniques for breaking grip, denying control, and getting out fast. The goal is always escape.

Module VI
Fight Enders

When running fails, the neck becomes your north star. The rear naked choke — the technique that saved Susan Kuhnhausen's life when the hitman her husband hired came for her. Works regardless of adrenaline, pain tolerance, or size.

Module VII
Surprise Attacks

What to do when someone grabs you from behind with no warning. Attacks over and under the arms, defending a rear choke attempt, multiple escape options. Because predators choose the moment your mind drifts.

Module VIII
Ground Defense — Part One

If you end up on the ground, the fight is not over. Technical standup, upkick, ankle sweep, triangle choke, hip bump sweep, kimura, and guillotine from guard. The ground is just another place to fight from.

Module IX
Ground Defense — Part Two

Pinned escapes, advanced strategy, and weapons defense. Bridge, bridge and roll, armbar from knee on belly, feigning compliance, the t-shirt choke, Ezekiel choke, and the upkick Terra Newell used to disarm her knife-wielding attacker.
